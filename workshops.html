STUDIO ETIQUETTE

Studio Safety & Guidelines

Help us keep a creative, clean environment. Here are basic guidelines for clay works.

Dress Comfortably

Clay washes out easily, but we suggest wearing old clothes and comfortable closed-toe shoes. Trim nails before throwing!

Reclaim Clay Bucket

Scraps of dried clay must go into our designated Reclaim Slurry buckets. We filter, wedge, and reuse raw clay to minimize waste.

Wipe Down Wheels

Please allow 15 minutes at the end of class to clean your wheel pan, rinse your sponge tools, and wipe stools for the next student.
